MEET THE BAND!

A lot of us have played music together before...but we decided we needed more horns!

This lead to the forming of DIDDY vs THE KINGDOM, a 9 piece ska influenced band from Leeds in the UK. The band is made up of friends from primary school, high school and beyond.

We share a love of energetic music and geek culture, having played video and board games together since a young age.

Most of us have gigged together in the past, but this is the first time some of us have come together for well over a decade.

And boy is it fun!
